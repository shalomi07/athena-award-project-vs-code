Let's learn indonesian Language!

Here are some simple greetings & daily conversation used in Indonesian, friendly for tourists :)

Indonesian English
Selamat pagi! Good morning!
Apa kabar? How are you?
Sampai jumpa! See you!
Siapa namamu? What is your name?
Terima kasih! Thank you!
Senang berkenalan/bertemu denganmu! Nice to meet you!
Permisi Excuse me
Maaf Sorry
Sampai jumpa! See you!
Di mana letak _____ (contoh: toilet)? Where is the ____ (example: toilet)?
Ayo pergi ke pantai! Let's go to the beach!
Saya mau pesan satu gelas kopi I'd like to order a glass of coffee
Restoran Restaurant
Taksi Taxi
Hotel Hotel

Fun fact: Indonesian language only use one grammar for all timing & situation (no past, present, future, continuous tenses)!
